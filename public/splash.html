chromeConnect
Take control of Chrome from your mobile device

Turn your touchable phone or tablet device into a mouse to control browsing in Chrome.

This extension captures gesture interactions taken from a paired mobile or tablet device, and incorporates the gesturing data into the navigation experience within your browser. ChromeConnect is an open source project and is currently in alpha testing phase.

Gestering Feature Support

One Finger Mouse with Tap Click

On the scroll tab use a one finger drag to move a synthetic mouse pointer around the page. Make sure to enable the pointer first from the settings in the extension drop down. A tap on your controller screen will create a click on any textlink or button within the DOM.

Pinch Zoom In / Out

From the pinch tab on your device a use normal pinch command to zoom into your page. Pinching out will return to the default browser zoom level. A tap on this screen will enable a quick zoom (turn off by performing a double tab command).

Two Finger Page Scroll

Use a two-finger scroll to move up and down the page. Three fingers up or down will bring you to the top / bottom of the page you are viewing.
